Why are Eucalyptus TENCEL™ sheets ideal for sleep?

EXCELLENT FOR SENSITIVE SKIN

GreenSecrets eucalyptus bedding is made from TENCEL™ lyocell wood-based fibers which offer exquisite softness, due to its sleek cross-section. It is naturally soft to the touch and offer long-lasting comfort. When viewed under an electron microscope, TENCEL™ Lyocell fibers exhibit a smooth surface area, producing a soft handfeel and ensuring super comfort for sensitive skins.

SUSTAINABLE

TENCEL™ Lyocell is created in an environmentally responsible, closed-loop production process, which transforms wood pulp into cellulosic fibers with high resource efficiency and low ecological impact. Our eucalyptus bedding sheets require 96% less water, 30% less energy, and zero pesticides or insecticides compared with cotton. GreenSecrets sheet bedding ship in plastic-free packaging

NO MORE NIGHT SWEATS

The Lyocell cellulose fibers have a higher vapor uptake than cotton combined with their smooth fiber surface; this supports the body’s natural thermal regulating mechanism, keeping your skin feeling cool and dry. Fabrics can also be engineered to provide warm and dry sensations on the skin. Get the best eucalyptus bedding sheets from Green Secrets.

DURABLE

TENCEL™ Lyocell fibers are versatile and distinguished by their high tenacity profile

ANTIBACTERIAL

TENCEL™ Lyocell fibers absorb moisture efficiently. Compared with polyester and synthetics, and even to cotton, there is less available moisture formed on the surface of the fiber for bacterial to grow. Consequently, the fiber provides a less favourable environment for bacterial growth. The high moisture absorption ability does not provide ambience for dust mites compared to cotton.
